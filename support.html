Orders & Shipping

Standard shipping: 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping: 1-2 business days. International shipping: 7-14 business days depending on destination.

Returns & Warranty

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Liquid-Glass bands have a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects.

Initiate a return from your account dashboard or contact support. We'll provide a return label and instructions.

The warranty covers manufacturing defects, self-healing failure (Liquid-Glass bands), and material defects. Normal wear and tear is not covered.

Product & Compatibility

Check your watch size on the back or in Settings > General > About. 38/40/41mm bands are compatible with each other, as are 42/44/45mm bands.

Clean with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals. Minor scratches will self-heal within 24-48 hours. Store away from extreme temperatures.

All bands are sweat and water-resistant. Liquid-Glass bands are fully waterproof up to 50 meters. We recommend removing bands before swimming.
