Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Apple Watches

The Apple Watch Series 9 is perfect for most users. The Ultra is designed for athletes and adventurers, while the SE offers great value for essential features.

Most bands are compatible within size categories: 38/40/41mm bands fit together, and 42/44/45mm bands fit together. The Apple Watch Ultra (49mm) uses 45mm bands.

GPS models require your iPhone nearby for calls and messages. Cellular models can connect directly to cellular networks, allowing you to use your watch independently.

Check the back of your Apple Watch or go to Settings > General > About on your watch. You can also measure your current band or use our size guide.
